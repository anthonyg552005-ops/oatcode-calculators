Mailchimp vs ConvertKit vs ActiveCampaign

Your Email List

Platform Comparison

Mailchimp

  • Best for: Beginners, e-commerce
  • Free tier: Up to 500 subscribers
  • Templates: 100+ email templates
  • Automation: Basic on paid plans
  • E-commerce: Excellent integrations

ConvertKit

  • Best for: Creators, bloggers
  • Free tier: Up to 1,000 subscribers
  • Landing pages: Built-in creator tools
  • Automation: Visual workflows
  • Simplicity: Clean, creator-focused UI

ActiveCampaign

  • Best for: Advanced automation, B2B
  • Free tier: 14-day trial only
  • Automation: Most powerful workflows
  • CRM: Built-in sales automation
  • Segmentation: Advanced tagging

Quick Recommendation

  • Mailchimp: Best if you're selling products (Shopify integration, product blocks)
  • ConvertKit: Best if you're a creator/blogger (landing pages, simple automation)
  • ActiveCampaign: Best if you need complex automation (advanced segmentation, CRM)

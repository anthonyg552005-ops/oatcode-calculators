📈 How to Make $2,000/Month Selling Canva Templates

The Math: To hit $2,000/month, you need either: (1) 20 templates selling 10 copies each at $10 = $2,000, or (2) 10 templates selling 20 copies at $10 = $2,000. It's very achievable.

Start with High-Demand Niches: Social media templates for Instagram coaches, real estate agents, and fitness influencers sell consistently. These groups need constant fresh content and will pay $12-18 for template packs.

Build a Template Library: Don't create one-offs. Your 20th template will make money while you sleep. Successful creators have 30-50+ templates generating passive income. Each template compounds your earnings.

Pinterest is Your Best Friend: 80% of Canva template traffic comes from Pinterest. Create 5-10 pins per template with clear mockups. Use Tailwind to schedule pins. One viral pin can generate $500-1,000 in sales.

Price for Volume or Premium: Either go high-volume ($8-12 templates selling 50-100x/month) or premium ($25-40 templates selling 10-20x/month). The middle ground ($15-20) often performs worst.

Bundle Everything: Once you have 10 templates, create bundles at 30% discount. A $120 bundle of 10 templates priced at $84 converts 3x better than individual sales. Buyers love feeling they got a deal.

Use Gumroad, Not Etsy: Etsy takes 6.5% + fees. Gumroad takes 10% but gives you email addresses for marketing. Owning your customer list is worth the extra 3.5%. Email past buyers when you launch new templates.

Automate Everything: Once created, templates sell 24/7. Set up Pinterest automation with Tailwind ($15/month), use Gumroad for instant delivery, and let ConvertKit email your list. You can make $2k/month working 5 hours/week.

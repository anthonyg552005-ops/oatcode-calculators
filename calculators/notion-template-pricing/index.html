How to Price Your Notion Templates

Pricing your Notion templates correctly is crucial for maximizing revenue while remaining competitive. The right price balances the value you provide with what your target audience is willing to pay.

Factors That Affect Template Pricing

  • Complexity: More databases, views, and automations = higher value
  • Target Audience: Business professionals pay more than students
  • Uniqueness: Novel solutions command premium pricing
  • Support & Updates: Ongoing support justifies higher prices
  • Competition: Research similar templates on Gumroad, Etsy, Notion marketplace

Notion Template Pricing Tiers

$5-15: Simple templates (habit trackers, reading lists, basic dashboards)

$15-35: Moderate templates (content planners, project management, CRM systems)

$35-75: Complex templates (business OS, complete productivity systems, financial trackers)

$75-200+: Pro templates (agency systems, advanced automations, niche professional tools)

How to Increase Template Value

  • Add video tutorials showing how to use the template
  • Include setup guides and documentation
  • Offer customization service for premium price tier
  • Provide lifetime updates and new features
  • Create community (Discord/Slack) for template users
  • Bundle multiple templates together

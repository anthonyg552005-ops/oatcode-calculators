💡 Shopify Plan Breakdown

Choosing the right Shopify plan can save you hundreds per month. The key is understanding transaction fees and feature requirements.

💳 Basic ($39/mo)

Best for: New stores under $5k/month. 2.9% + 30¢ online card rate. Basic reports. Good starter plan.

🏪 Shopify ($105/mo)

Best for: Growing stores $5k-50k/month. 2.6% + 30¢ card rate. Professional reports. Gift cards included.

📈 Advanced ($399/mo)

Best for: Established stores $50k+/month. 2.4% + 30¢ card rate. Advanced reports. Third-party shipping rates.

🚀 Plus ($2,300/mo)

Best for: Enterprise stores $500k+/month. Dedicated support, unlimited staff, advanced automation. Custom pricing.

💰 How to Choose the Right Shopify Plan

Transaction Fees Are the Key: Basic plan charges 2.9% + 30¢, Shopify charges 2.6% + 30¢, Advanced charges 2.4% + 30¢. At $10k/month revenue, you save $30/month going from Basic to Shopify. At $50k/month, you save $150/month going to Advanced. The higher plan pays for itself.

Use Shopify Payments: If you use third-party payment gateways (Stripe, PayPal), Shopify adds 2% extra transaction fee on Basic, 1% on Shopify, 0.5% on Advanced. This adds up fast. Use Shopify Payments to avoid these fees entirely.

Calculate Your Break-Even: Shopify plan costs $66/month more than Basic. At 2.6% vs 2.9% card rate (0.3% savings), you break even at $22k/month revenue. Above $22k/month, Shopify plan is cheaper. Above $100k/month, Advanced is cheaper.

Annual Billing Saves 25%: All Shopify plans offer 25% discount on annual billing. Basic drops to $29/month, Shopify to $79/month, Advanced to $299/month. If you're committed, this is a no-brainer.

Don't Pay for Features You Don't Need: Advanced Reports on Shopify plan are only useful if you check analytics weekly. Third-Party Shipping Rates on Advanced only matter if you ship from multiple warehouses. Most stores don't need these features.

Start with Basic, Upgrade at $10k/Month: If you're just starting, Basic is fine. Once you hit $10k/month consistently for 3 months, upgrade to Shopify plan. At $75k/month, upgrade to Advanced. Let your revenue dictate the upgrade, not features.
